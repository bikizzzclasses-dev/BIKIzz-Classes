Meet Our Founder

Biki Bharali

Founder & Mathematics Mentor

Biki Bharali is the founder of BIKIzz' Classes, a Mathematics learning platform established with the vision of making Mathematics simple engaging, and accessible to every student. Believing that Mathematics is more than just formulas and calculations, he founded the platform to help students overcome their fear of the subject, develop logical thinking, and build strong conceptual understanding. Driven by a passion for teaching and mentoring, his goal is to create a collaborative learning environment where students can learn with confidence, ask questions freely, and discover the beauty of Mathematics. Through BIKIzz' Classes, he aims to inspire young minds and make quality Mathematics education accessible to all.
